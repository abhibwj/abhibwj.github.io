Abhishek Bhardwaj

Assistant Professor of Finance
Indian School of Business

I am a financial economist with a Ph.D. from NYU Stern School of Business. My research examines how financial institutions shape credit allocation, risk transmission, and economic outcomes. I focus on banking, private equity, and non-bank finance, as well as how firms adapt to climate change and natural disasters.
